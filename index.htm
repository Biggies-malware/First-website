CCTV Camera Installation

We specialize in the professional installation of high-quality CCTV cameras for both indoor and outdoor surveillance, ensuring maximum security for homes, offices, schools, and businesses.

Types of Cameras We Install:

  • Dome Cameras: Ideal for indoor surveillance. They are discreet, resistant to tampering, and provide a wide field of view.
    • Resolution: 1080p / 4K
    • Night Vision: Up to 30m
    • Fixed or Varifocal Lenses
  • PTZ Cameras (Pan-Tilt-Zoom): Suitable for large areas like parking lots and building perimeters.
    • Remote control via app or PC
    • Auto-tracking of moving objects
    • Optical zoom up to 36x
    • Weatherproof IP66 rating
  • Bullet Cameras: Best for outdoor use. Provide long-distance viewing and are highly visible as a deterrent.
    • Weatherproof casing
    • Wide Dynamic Range (WDR)
    • Infrared LEDs for night vision
